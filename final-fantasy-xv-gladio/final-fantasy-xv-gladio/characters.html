GLADIOLUS AMICITIA

The Amicita family has long served as the shield protecting the kings of Lucis and their household,
and Gladiolus is its eldest son. The bond he shares with Noctis trancends that of a bodyguard and
his liege. With his prodigious strenght, he stands ever ready to keep his companions from harm.

NOCTIS LUCIS CAELUM

Heir apparent to the Lucian throne, Noctis's trials begin when he sets forth from the crown city in
order to wed the Lady Lunafreya Nox Fleuret. In combat, he weilds spectral weapons which he
forges from thin air, a power possessed by those of his royal line

IGNIS SCIENTIA

Ignis was raised alongside Prince Noctis to be adviser for the heir apparent. An intensive education
instilled in him the resourcefulness and composure required for the role, his tactical acumen proving
invaluable over the course of the prince's journey.

PROMTO ARGENTUM

Fast friends with Noctis since they met as teenagers, Promto is a young man of common birth
who finds himself out of his depth when tradegy befalls Lucis. Nevertheless, he struves to carry
more than his own weight, lightening his companions' burdens as well as their spirits.

IRIS AMICITIA

Gladio's little sister and long-time friend of Noctis and the others. She fled the Crown City during the
empire's attack and has taken refuge in Lestallum. But growing up after Noctis reuniting with the crystal
she decided to become a daemon slayer to protect the people of Lucis from the ever-lasting night.

GILGAMESH

Friend and rival of Gladiolus, this master of the art of war is always on a search after the legendary
sword Excalibur. Although his dorky attitude might be make him seem weak, he is an outmost deadly combatant,
giving Gladio a hard time in their first fight and permenantly scaring him.

ARDYN IZUNIA

Chacellor of Niflheim. Ardyn rose to power by introducing magitek infantry to the ranks of the
imperial army, and has since amassed political power at the emperor's side. An aloof manner
shrouds the chacellor in mystery.
