Story

2 years after the events of Final Fantasy XV, Gladiolus has found
him self alone, stripped of his friends and family he desperatly
searches for meaning to his life in this newly saved Lucis. But as the heir
to the emperor starts another civil war, Gladiolus cannot help but to get
dragged into another conflict. But as soon as he looses hope, he hears
something on the battlefield, a rumor of sightenings of his old friends.
Now it's up to you to help Gladio reunite with his long lost
friends.
